Makeup Inspiration

Overview:

The glowy light makeup look is great for everyday wear or sunset pics. This look will make you appear warm, bronzy, and radiant.

  1. Start with a hydrating primer for smooth, even skin.
  2. Apply a light to medium-coverage foundation and blend well with a beauty sponge.
  3. Use a warm-toned eyeshadow palette (browns, golds, and soft peach shades) to create depth on the eyes.
  4. Add a coat of lengthening mascara.
  5. Lightly contour the cheeks and nose, then add a peach or rose-toned blush.
  6. Finish with a natural nude or mauve lipstick and a touch of highlighter on the cheekbones.

Overview:

The smoky eye look is bold, dramatic, and perfect for nights out or special occasions. It focuses on blending deep shades to create a sultry, eye-catching effect while keeping the rest of the face balanced and polished.

  1. Start with an eyeshadow primer to ensure your look lasts all night.
  2. Apply a neutral transition shade to the crease for smooth blending.
  3. Pack a dark eyeshadow shade (black, charcoal, or deep brown) onto the lid and blend outward.
  4. Smudge the same dark shade along the lower lash line for intensity.
  5. Add eyeliner to the waterline and a sharp wing if desired.
  6. Apply multiple coats of volumizing mascara or finish with false lashes.

Tip:Keep the lips neutral with a nude lipstick or gloss to let the eyes stand out.
